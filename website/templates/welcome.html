Early Days' overarching aim is providing an application that will aid individuals as they navigate the early stages of getting to know a potential partner.

We do so through an engaging questionnaire that encapsulates a series of prompts to discover what dealbreakers may exist for a particular person to prevent them from entering a relationship.

Our Mission

Early Days' mission is to guide users through an engaging process to indentify personal relationship dealbreakers and preferences. In an era of increasingly fast-paced dating dynamics, navigating the early stages of getting to know a potential partner has its complexities, particularly when it comes to assessing compatibility with those prospective partners. Early Days aims to provide a solution to this issue so that everyone can feel empowered to determine their compatibility and acknowledge dealbreakers shamelessly.
